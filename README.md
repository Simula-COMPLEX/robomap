# Software Engineering for Self-Adaptive Robotics: A Research Agenda

[![License: CC BY 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC_BY_4.0-lightgrey.svg)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
[![Status: Research](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Research-0d9488.svg)]()

This repository hosts the interactive companion webpage for the research paper **"Software Engineering for Self-Adaptive Robotics: A Research Agenda."** 🔗 **[View the Interactive Dashboard](https://simula-complex.github.io/robomap/)**

It provides an interactive and searchable mapping of research challenges to critical quality aspects (Safety, Reliability, Performance, Security) and self-adaptive robotic characteristics.

## 📄 Research Paper

This work is part of the 2030 research agenda established by an international group of researchers to define the future of robotic software engineering.

**Title:** Software Engineering for Self-Adaptive Robotics: A Research Agenda  
**Authors:** Hassan Sartaj, Shaukat Ali, Ana Cavalcanti, Lukas Esterle, Cláudio Gomes, Peter Gorm Larsen, Anastasios Tefas, Jim Woodcock, and Houxiang Zhang  
**Year:** 2025  
**Preprint:** [arXiv:2505.19629](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.19629)
